ChordFracture

ChordFracture is a guitar chord form manager built for alternate tunings.

Create and edit tunings (e.g., DADGAD, CGDGCD), tap the interactive fretboard to save chord forms, and organize everything with tags.

Find your ideas fast with powerful search by name, tuning, fret positions, memo, or tags. Switch between list and detail views, and keep track with created/updated timestamps. Works offline, supports dark mode, and is localized in English and Japanese.

Features

  • Alternate tuning management (create, edit, tag)
  • Tap the fretboard to capture chord forms
  • Tag both tunings and chord forms
  • Instant search: name, tuning, fret positions, memo, tags
  • List/detail views, created/updated timestamps
  • Offline storage and dark mode
  • English/Japanese localization
  • iPhone/iPad support
